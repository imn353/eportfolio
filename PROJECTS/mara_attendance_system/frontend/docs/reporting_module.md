# Reporting Module

The Reporting Module provides high-level attendance analytics and audit logs to institutional leaders. The level of data visible depends strictly on the user's role.

## Role-Based Visibility

1. **Admin (System-Wide Reports)**
   - **Access Level:** Unrestricted.
   - **Capabilities:** Can view data across all programs, cohorts, and subjects.
   - **Key Features:** System Attendance Trend graphs, Institutional Summaries (Total Enrollment, Total Absences), and full Audit Logs for every class session.

2. **Head of Department (Department Reports)**
   - **Access Level:** Restricted to their specific department or program domain.
   - **Capabilities:** Can view aggregated attendance trends and audit logs *only* for the subjects and classes within their purview.
   - **Key Features:** Department-level trend charts, domain-specific summaries, and localized audit logs.

3. **Lecturers (Class Dashboard)**
   - **Access Level:** Restricted to assigned classes.
   - **Capabilities:** Lecturers do not have access to the global "Reports" module. Instead, their analytics are contained within their personal Dashboard and Discipline pages, focused strictly on their own students.

## Key Features

- **Dynamic Filtering:** Users can drill down into the data using multi-select filters for Time periods (e.g., Last 30 Days), specific Subjects, Classes, and Lecturers.
- **Trend Analysis:** A visual line chart displays the attendance percentage trend over the selected time period, allowing leadership to spot declining attendance before it becomes a systemic issue.
- **Audit Logging:** A detailed timeline of all submitted attendance sessions, highlighting the specific class, lecturer, and any late or absent students.
- **PDF Export:** Leadership can export the current dashboard view—including all applied filters and trend data—into a clean, printable PDF report for institutional meetings.
